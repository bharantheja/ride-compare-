About RideCompare

Built for Bengaluru commuters

Every day, millions of people in Bengaluru waste time switching between 5+ ride apps just to find the cheapest fare. RideCompare fixes that — compare all prices in under 3 seconds.

All fare data is sourced from official 2025 Karnataka rate cards. No login required, no ads, completely free.
